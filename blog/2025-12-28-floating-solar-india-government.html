Floating Solar in India: Government Push and Projects

28 Dec 2025

Floating solar is gaining momentum in India as states and central agencies look to use water bodies for generation without consuming land. Several tenders have been floated for reservoirs, dams, and canals.

Pilot projects and larger installations are under way in states including Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Maharashtra. We look at the policy push, typical project sizes, and what developers and EPC players need to know about floating PV.
