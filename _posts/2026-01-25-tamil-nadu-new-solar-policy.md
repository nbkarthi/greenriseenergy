Tamil Nadu has released an updated solar policy with clearer targets for rooftop, open access, and utility-scale capacity. The policy emphasises faster approvals, group net metering for housing societies, and support for MSMEs and industries.

Rooftop subsidies are aligned with the central PM Surya Ghar scheme. For open access, the state has simplified banking and wheeling provisions. Here are the highlights that matter most for developers and consumers.
