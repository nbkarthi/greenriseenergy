The Approved List of Models and Manufacturers (ALMM) for solar PV modules has been updated again. New domestic and overseas manufacturers have been added, and some models have been removed. Projects under government schemes and many state tenders must use ALMM-listed modules.

For EPC contractors and developers, staying on top of ALMM is essential to avoid delays and compliance issues. We track the latest list and eligibility for PM Surya Ghar and other subsidised programmes.
