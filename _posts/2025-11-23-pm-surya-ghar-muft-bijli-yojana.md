The PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana continues to drive residential rooftop solar with central financial support. Eligibility, subsidy tiers, and the application process have been refined. Beneficiaries can get substantial upfront support for systems up to 3 kW and beyond.

We outline the latest guidelines, how to apply through the national portal, and how the scheme dovetails with state policies in Tamil Nadu and other states.
