Module and balance-of-system costs have continued to ease in India over the last few months. Factors include higher domestic manufacturing, ALMM expansion, and competitive imports in segments where they are permitted.

Rooftop system prices (₹/W) have moved down for both residential and C&I segments. We look at the main drivers and what to expect for the next quarter when planning a solar installation.
