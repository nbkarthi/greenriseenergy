Tamil Nadu has revised its net metering framework to align with national guidelines and support more rooftop capacity. Key changes include simplified approval flows, clarity on banking and settlement, and expanded group net metering for housing societies and industries.

For homeowners and MSMEs, the updated policy makes it easier to size systems and get surplus energy credited. We summarise what changed and how to apply under the new rules.
