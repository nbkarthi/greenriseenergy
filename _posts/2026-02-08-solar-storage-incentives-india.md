Central and state governments are stepping up support for solar combined with battery storage. New guidelines and pilot schemes aim to improve grid stability and enable round-the-clock renewable supply for C&I and discoms.

Tamil Nadu and other states have expressed interest in storage-linked solar tenders. For businesses weighing backup and peak-shaving, incentive-linked storage is becoming more relevant. We summarise the latest policy and pilot updates.
