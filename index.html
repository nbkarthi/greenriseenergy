Our Process

How We Work

A streamlined 5-step approach from initial consultation to long-term performance monitoring.

1

Consultation & Site Assessment

We analyze your energy consumption, roof condition, shading analysis, and solar potential to determine the optimal system size and configuration for your property.

2

Custom Design & Proposal

Our engineers create a detailed system design using advanced PV modeling software. You receive a comprehensive proposal with performance estimates, ROI analysis, and financing options.

3

Engineering & Procurement

We handle all engineering drawings, permitting, and procure Tier-1 solar panels, inverters, and mounting systems from trusted manufacturers with full warranties.

4

Professional Installation

Our certified installation teams execute the project with precision, adhering to safety standards and best practices. Typical residential installations complete in 1-3 days.

5

Commissioning & Monitoring

After grid connection and system testing, we provide training and set up real-time monitoring. You can track energy production anytime via our mobile app.
